Department of Physics

We're dedicated to excellence in research, teaching and service. Our faculty experts prepare undergraduate and graduate students for careers in physics, astronomy and a range of related fields.

Our faculty and student researchers collaborate in labs across the university and beyond, contributing to projects and discoveries in many physics subfields.
